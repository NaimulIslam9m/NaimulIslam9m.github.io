So, you want to get into cybersecurity? First you have to develop “Hacker Mindset”. A hacker mindset is a highly curious mind like a child. Constantly wondering how things work. What will happen if I do this or that. Trying to break things to understand the inner workings.

You can see Hacking Google series by Google to get an idea about cybersecurity and how security measures works at Google.

Fundamental

You are not a student of computer science and have little to no knowledge about computers then you should do Harvard University’s CS50: Introduction to Computer Science course.

After completing CS50, go for Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. It is an 8-course series certification program by Google that will clear all the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity with hands-on exercise.

After that do Cybersecurity for Students learning path by Letsdefend. It is a good resource to have an overview of fundamental concepts of cybersecurity like networking, windows, linux, cryptography, malware analysis and job hunting.

After doing following three course you will have a strong foundation on computer, network and security:

  • CompTIA 220-1001 and 220-1002 A+ Course by Professor Messer
  • CompTIA N10-008 Network+ Course by Professor Messer
  • CompTIA SY0-601 Security+ Course by Professor Messer

Now we need to understand the how things works a little deeper before proceed any further. So for that you need to do and practice the following course if you can (not free):

  • Fundamentals of Network Engineering
  • Fundamentals of Database Engineering
  • Fundamentals of Backend Engineering

OK. So you have an idea what cybersecurity is. Now watch this video subscribe all the youtube channels mentioned bellow:

  • Heath Adam’s post on active hacktubers 2023.
  • Top 20 bug bounty YouTube channels in 2022

Now let’s refresh some concepts again:

  • Linux: Watch and practice Linux Essentials for Ethical Hackers by Freecodecamp.
  • Python: Watch and practice Python for Hackers course by PhD Security.
  • SQL: Watch and practice SQL Injection course by Freecodecamp.

Certification

If you have done Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. So you can skip both certificate below:

  • Certified in Cybersecurity by ISC2 is a beginner level certification which certifies your foundational knowledge, skills and abilities for an entry or junior level cybersecurity role. Also, till now it is FREE.
  • Comptia Security+ is also a beginner level certification which validates the baseline skills necessary to perform core security functions and pursue an IT security career. You need to spend $219 USD (in Bangladesh) to do the exam.
